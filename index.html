ℹ️ About This Project
�

What Is This?

A collection of automated monitors that scan free, public government data (SEC EDGAR, USPTO, FDA) for actionable financial signals. Everything here is completely free and runs on a $100 Raspberry Pi. No API keys needed.

�

Why Does This Work?

The information is already public and free. The advantage is automation. The SEC receives 30,000+ filings daily. No human can read them all. Our monitors scan every 3 hours and flag the ones that historically predict stock movements.

⚠️

Risk Disclaimer

Every signal has a chance of failure. This is educational and research only — NOT financial advice. Always do your own research. Never invest more than you can afford to lose. The "potential earnings" shown are optimistic estimates, not guarantees.

�️

How It Works

Step 1: Monitor fetches daily index from SEC EDGAR
Step 2: Filters out ETF/fund/routine filings
Step 3: Downloads actual filings (parallel)
Step 4: Scores materiality via keywords + structure
Step 5: Updates this dashboard with new signals
